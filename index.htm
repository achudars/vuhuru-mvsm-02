How To

Step 1 - Create Your Sitemap

Login to www.MyVideoSitemap.com and click on Create Video Sitemap

Step 2 - Enter the Details of Your Sitemap

  • Sitemap title - Give your sitemap a name so you can identify it; this will only be used in your account and does not affect your website listing
  • URL - Enter the full domain name (including www.) that the sitemap is for

  • Video Location - Enter the webpage where your video is embedded
  • Title - Enter a title for your video, using keywords here will help with SEO
  • Thumbnail location - If you want an image to show in the search rankings, upload one to a hosting account and enter it's URL here
  • Description - Enter a description for your video, using keywords here will help with SEO
  • Publication date - If your video has a future launch date, enter it here for rankings to begin from this date
  • Expiration date - If you want your video to stop ranking at a future date, enter it here
  • You can add more videos by clicking on the Add More Video button
  • When you have added all the videos you want to, click Save

Step 3 - Download Your SiteMap

When you have saved your sitemap, you will see a list of your existing sitemaps

Download your sitemap to your computer

Step 4 - Upload Your SiteMap

Sign into your Google Webmaster Tools account, and submit your sitemap

You only need to do this once as Google will continue to check for updates to the sitemap once it has been submitted

Step 5 - Submit Your SiteMap to Google Webmaster Tools

Step 6 - Wait for Your Video to Rank!

FAQ

Why do I need a VideoSitemap?

Google and other search engines have added a separate database for video searches which are often listed in the top ten results. By having a Google Authorised VideoSitemap your videos will show up in Google Results.

Will my video rank high in Google Search?

Yes, if your video is a good match for the Keywords and Keyphrases of your audience and you consider SEO when creating the metadata for your videos. Google have been giving preference to video results for over 4 years!

How long till Google processes MyVideoSitemap?

It may take some time for your Sitemap to be processed. You can see if it's been processed correctly by going to video.google.com and perform a site-restrict query, like this: [site:mydomain.com]. This will show all videos that match the domain.
